How to play

  • Collect dots to keep up your energy level and continue playing
  • Avoid sharp deadly enemies or pick big dots to power-ups and kill them
  • Collect coins and rare gems to buy upgrades

Upgrades

  • Longer power-up
  • Less energy consumption
  • Move faster around the page
  • Attract energy dots
  • Repulse enemies
  • Reduce game speed
